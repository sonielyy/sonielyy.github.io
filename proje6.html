Student & School Information Interface

This is a static front-end project built to simulate the web interface of a private high school management system. It features various UI sections such as student dashboards, course listings, schedules, and staff profiles ‚Äî all designed with clarity and usability in mind.

Why did you do this project?

Administrative dashboards are common in education tech. I wanted to practice designing such systems and focusing on layout consistency, user-friendly navigation, and the visual organization of educational content.

What was your contribution to this project?

I developed all UI components with only HTML and CSS. I designed the layout structure, created sample data placeholders, and focused on presenting information in a clean and organized way without backend integration.

What was the most challenging part, and how did you overcome it?

The main challenge was designing a multi-section layout that remained clean and readable, especially with large blocks of data. I used tables, card layouts, and consistent spacing to solve this issue and avoid clutter.
